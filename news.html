🕊️ Raila Odinga Passes Away at 80

Kenya mourns the loss of Raila Odinga, a towering figure in African politics and a champion of democratic reform. He died in India on October 15, 2025, following a cardiac arrest during treatment.

His passing sparked nationwide tributes, with thousands gathering in Bondo, Kisumu, and Kibera to honor his legacy. Businesses paused, streets filled with green leaves, and voices rose in remembrance of a leader who shaped generations.
