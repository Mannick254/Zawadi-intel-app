Nickson Ochieng Okoth, born on April 9, 20's, in Elwanda, Butula Constituency, Busia, Kenya, is a dedicated and highly skilled professional with expertise in security management, access control, cybersecurity, technical troubleshooting, and server-side development. Raised in a close-knit Luhya family alongside four sisters and one brother, he embodies values of teamwork, humility, and perseverance.

His education journey began at Lugulu AC Primary School and continued at Malanga High School, where he excelled in his KCSE exams. He later earned certifications in AED, CPR, Fire Fighting, and First Aid through Rainbow Patriots. Professionally, Nickson served as a Security Guard at Firstline Security and now works as an Access Control Specialist at Rainbow Patriots Security, promoting strategic planning and safeguarding critical resources.

Beyond security, Nickson has cultivated expertise in ethical hacking (PowerShell), server-side development (Node.js), and system optimization. He also explores creative passions in DJing and sound mixing (VirtualDJ). Fluent in English and Kiswahili, he is deeply inspired by action-packed and spy thriller films, cybersecurity advancements, and music. His Christian faith grounds his pursuit of excellence and personal growth.
